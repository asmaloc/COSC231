General Information

Greninja is the final evolution of the water starter in the Kalos region, Froakie. Froakie and Frogadier are pure water types, but Greninja gains a secondary dark type. Greninja is based on a frog; spiked protrusions above its eyes inspired by horned frogs, coloration and body shape similar to splendid leaf frog, and bubble-like protrusions referencing traditional myth of toads causing warts. Other frog Pokémon are the Poliwag evolutionary lines and the Tympole evolutionary line. Greninja is also a based on a ninja. Japan associates frogs and toads with ninjas based on the folk story "The Tale of the Gallant Jiraiya." The only other ninja Pokémon is Ninjask, a bug/flying duo type.

Strengths

  • Greninja has the highest base speed of all Water-type Pokémon.
  • Protean is Greninja's hidden ability. Before the Pokémon uses a move, it becomes a pure Pokémon of that type.
  • Functions as a superb suicide lead, thanks to Spikes and Toxic Spikes and Protean Shadow Sneak.
  • Greninja's movepool is vast, containing many types of attacks and status moves.
  • Greninja will have a third possible ability, Battle Bond, which mega-evolves without using the item slot.
  • The only Pokémon that can learn the protective fighting type move, Mat Block, is Greninja.
  • Greninja can cover its type weaknesses with Gunk Shot, Extrasensory, Grass Knot, Ice Beam, and Dig.
  • Greninja can learn the priority attack, Water Shuriken.

Weaknesses

  • Greninja has mediocre offensive stats and bulk.
  • Darkrai outspeeds Greninja and puts it to sleep with Dark Void, preventing hazard setups.
  • Pokémon with the ability Magic Bounce will bounce back entry hazards and beat it with attacks.
  • Faster Pokémon that use the move Taunt can prevent Greninja from using hazards.
  • Greninja is frail, so priority moves, such as Extreme Speed, are devastating.
  • Bulky fighting and fairy type Pokémon can wall Greninja.
  • Blast Burn.

Trivia

  • Greninja is the ninth Pokémon to have been playable in Super Smash Bros. (Counting Squirtle, Ivysaur, and Charizard in Brawl as separate).
  • It creates throwing stars out of compressed water. When it spins them and throws them at high speed, these stars can split metal in two.
  • Greninja is 4'11" and weighs 88.2 lbs.
  • What looks like a scarf is actually a tongue.
  • Greninja is a combination of grenouille (French for frog) and ninja.
